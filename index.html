How to play?

Instructions
  • Insert your name and hit search!
  • search will put you on the map so wait calmly until a new player joins the session
  • Only 2 players can play at a time
  • Once matched to a second player, the game begins
  • select your choice from the list of options below (rock-paper-scissors)
  • wait until other player makes a choice
  • once the 2 players picked their options results will be display
  • check the score board in the middle of the screen to see your wins/losses

    • Chat
  • located on your right side you will find a message icon, click to start chatting with your opponent!
